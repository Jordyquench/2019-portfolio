About Me

Who am I?

Hi there, my name is Jordan Gault, I’m currently studying BDES (HONS) Interaction design at the Belfast school of art. I am in my final year of the course, currently working on my major project (You can see how I am getting on by viewing my "Touched With Contempt" case study). I am also a UX Designer at Origin Digital, based in Holywood.

I have always had a passion for design in all aspects, everything from UI and UX to branding and illustration. But all ideas have to stem from something and I have the capabilities to come up with forward-thinking ideas. This mindset gives me the ability to have an open mind when it comes to design thinking.

I also see myself as a storyteller, all design is telling a story, with the right concept and tools your story can be brought to life in unique and informative ways.
